glimpse draft

two players

  • 9 packs of 15 cards per drafter
  • the drafters each crack one pack, then make 1 pick
  • each drafter then removes 2 cards from their packs from the draft (i.e. burns 2 cards)
  • drafters switch packs with their opponent
  • repeat this process until every pack has been drafted and each drafter has a 45 card pool
  • build a 40 card deck, and get to playing
